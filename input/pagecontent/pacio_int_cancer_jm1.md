
### Background

This use case describes a cancer related use case for demonstrating a PACIO integration track.

### Persona

[`Patient Jenny Mosley`](Patient-jennymosley01-patient.html) is a 68 year black female with a past medical history significant for [`depression`](Condition-jennymosley01-condition-depression-01.html), a 20-pack-year history of smoking (current smoker), anxiety, and [`hypertension`](Condition-jennymosley01-condition-hypertension-01.html). Her family history was significant for a maternal aunt with ovarian cancer at age 69, a sister with breast cancer at age 64, and deceased paternal uncle due to pancreatic cancer. ...*etc*..
